Our Services

Comprehensive construction and design solutions tailored to your needs.

Turnkey Project

Need to modernize or improve an old or rundown building, office space or residential unit? Our team is equipped with the expertise to advise and work with you through the process, while adhering to required building regulations. Work ranges from extensions, loft conversions, redesigning of floor plans or electrical and plumbing re-routing etc.

Interior Fit-out

A space is only an empty space until it is transformed to accommodate the vision and specific requirements of its tenant. We work closely with our team of partner architects, interior designers, suppliers as well as our experienced installation teams to bring your vision into a reality. Be it lighting design, space conceptualization or even to choosing the right interior colour schemes and furniture for your space.

Carpentry Work

Carpentry and joinery work have been one of the core business of Boon Woh Group Company for 50 years. We provide customized carpentry services to cater to your design and interior. Our factory and skilled carpenters are able to produce quality work within a short turnaround time, without any compromise on the quality.

Electrical Wiring Installation

Electrical wiring installation or electrical rewiring is a key process in any renovation or building work. Be it new installations repair work, extensions of wiring or even solely for maintenance of existing electrical work. Installations and testing will be done by licensed electricians, certified by EMA.

General Building

There are many elements to building construction and it is an intricate process. B+W Builders are certified general builders by BCA and we pride ourselves on delivering quality work. Our experienced project team work closely with architects, relevant authorities as well as all sub-contractors to ensure that your project is completed according to the project timeline and well within the project budget.

Interior Design & Finishing Works

Experienced in all interior decoration and finishing works, we are certified by BCA and have undertaken numerous A&A works from the industrial, commercial or residential sectors. These comprise of repairs, renovations and maintenance works and all the finishing that come with it. Our team works closely with interior designers and architects who are specialized in the field to deliver quality work according to the market standards, and to your expectations.
