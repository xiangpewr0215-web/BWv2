Our Story

Innovation, precision and careful attention to detail forms the foundation of the team at B+W Builders with a history spanning 50 years. From humble beginnings in woodworking, supplying a multitude of industries in Singapore with custom made furniture and wood finishing, to a company providing a comprehensive range of construction management services.

B+W has established itself as an innovator in construction, renovation and interior fit-out works to many sectors. It is our belief that through forging strong partnerships with our network of suppliers and specialist tradespeople, we are able to combine traditional and modern manufacturing fabrication techniques, providing you with up to date solutions.

Our team at B+W are a passionate bunch who pride themselves in the work that we do, embracing a pragmatic approach to providing solutions to your construction ideals. Our aim is to be your partner in assisting you to bridge your ideas to actualization in timely fashion.
